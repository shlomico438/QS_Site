About QuickScribe

Founded in 2026 in Tel Aviv, Israel, QuickScribe is an AI-native transcription and documentation platform. We deliver highly accurate speech-to-text experiences in languages and professional domains where generic models fall short. By combining advanced speech recognition, language-specific optimization, and cloud-native AWS infrastructure (including Lambda and S3), we securely transform audio and video recordings into structured, actionable text for meetings, content creation, and research workflows.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver fast, secure, and highly accurate domain-specific transcription that removes localized language barriers.

In healthcare, our goal is deeply personal: to liberate clinicians from their keyboards, allowing medical professionals to look into the eyes of their patients instead of staring at a screen.

Leadership

Shlomi Cohen

Founder & CEO

Shlomi Cohen is a technology leader with more than 15 years of experience in communications, wireless systems, IoT, embedded platforms, and voice technologies.

Before founding QuickScribe, Shlomi held senior engineering and architecture roles at DSP Group, Altair Semiconductor, and Alvarion Technologies, where he led the development of innovative, globally deployed smart voice and wireless solutions. He has contributed to multiple patented technologies across voice systems and communication platforms.

At QuickScribe, Shlomi leads product strategy, AI architecture, cloud infrastructure, and the development of language-specific transcription technologies designed to maximize accuracy and usability for professional users.
